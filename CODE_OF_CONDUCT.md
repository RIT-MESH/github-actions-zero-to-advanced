# Code of Conduct

We follow the Contributor Covenant 2.1.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Standards

**Expected:** kindness, empathy, patience, welcoming questions from beginners, giving graceful feedback, accepting responsibility, focusing on what is best for the community.

**Unacceptable:** sexualized language, trolling, insults, public or private harassment, publishing others' private information without consent, other unethical or unprofessional conduct.

## Enforcement

Problems? Contact the repository maintainer via the **Report content** option on GitHub. Reports are reviewed privately and result in fair, proportionate responses.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
